Benefits:

Dates will provide you with the longest satiety.
It’s also rich with vitamin B5 that will increase your endurance.
With 282 kcal per every 100 g of the product, it’s a good snack for those who want to
prevent overeating and one of the most beneficial dried fruits for weight loss. 

Some best ways to eat dates:
 Add dates to salads. Chopped dates are an excellent addition to traditional green salads, 
grain salads,other vegetable medleys.
For a salad with 4-6 servings, try adding 5-8 chopped dates (depending on their size).
Some recipes ideas include:
Roasted cauliflower salad with chopped dried dates.
Butternut squash and quinoa salad with dates

Blend dates into a smoothie. Dates are an excellent way to sweeten foods without adding refined sugar.
Soak dried dates in hot water for 10 minutes, then add them to your favorite smoothies. 
For 1 serving, try using about 3 pitted dates . 
Some smoothie ideas include:
Peanut butter and jelly date smoothie
Banana date smoothie

Eat dried dates for an easy snack. You can pop dried dates right into 
your mouth for a quick and delicious snack.
